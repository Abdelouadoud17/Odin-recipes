Banana Pancakes

Description

These banana pancakes are soft, fluffy, and easy to make. They are perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Made with ripe bananas and simple ingredients, this recipe is both delicious and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas (1 for the batter, 1 for topping)
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup of flour
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Mash one banana in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Add the egg and mix well.
  3. Add flour, milk, baking powder, and salt, then mix until combined.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little butter or oil.
  5. Pour some batter into the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Slice the second banana.
  8. Serve the pancakes topped with banana slices and honey.
