Scrambled Eggs with Toast

Description

Scrambled eggs with toast is a classic and comforting breakfast that is quick to prepare. Itâ€™s filling, nutritious, and perfect for anyone learning how to cook simple meals.

This dish requires very few ingredients and can be ready in just a few minutes. You can enjoy it as is or customize it by adding vegetables, cheese, or herbs for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoonbutter
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add milk, salt, and pepper, then mix well.
  3. Heat a pan over low to medium heat and add butter.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  5. Stir slowly until the eggs are soft and cooked.
  6. Toast the bread slices.
  7. Serve the scrambled eggs hot with toast.
