Fresh Garden Salad

Description

This fresh garden salad is light, healthy, and refreshing. Itâ€™s perfect as a side dish or a quick meal on a hot day.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables well.
  2. Chop the lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion.
  3. Put everything into a large bowl.
  4. Add olive oil, lemon juice, and salt.
  5. Mix gently until everything is coated.
  6. Serve fresh.
